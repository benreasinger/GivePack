GivePak creates premium backpacks from recycled ocean plastics and donates part of profits to projects that restore coastlines, equip students, and build resilient communities.

Our Mission

"GivePak creates high-quality, sustainable backpacks made from recycled ocean plastics, giving consumers the power to make a difference with every purchase. Through our profit-sharing model, we invest in causes like clean water access, education, and reforestation to create lasting, positive changes in communities around the world."

Vision

Equip the future learners of the world with high-quality, sustainable backpacks that utilize ocean plastics and build a better future.

What we do

Design and produce durable backpacks from recycled ocean plastics and partner with nonprofits to maximize social and environmental impact.

How we do it

Profit-sharing model, transparent partnerships, and measurable impact targets focused on coastal cleanup and student support.

Why it matters

Every backpack sold removes plastic from the ocean, funds community projects, and empowers people with tools for success.
